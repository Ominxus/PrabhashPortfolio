ClipdNow

ClipdNow is my short-form content brand where I create fast, engaging, and trend-focused videos for platforms like TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

The brand focuses on viral creator moments, entertainment clips, sports reactions, gaming edits, and story-based short-form content. Through this project, I improved my skills in video editing, trend research, audience retention, captions, branding, and analytics.
